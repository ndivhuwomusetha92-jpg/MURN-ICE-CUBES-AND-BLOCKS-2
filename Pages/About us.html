About Us

Building a stronger community, one block at a time.

Who We Are

MURN ICE CUBES & BLOCKS FACTORY is a proudly Venda-based company dedicated to producing high-quality ice cubes and blocks for households, events, restaurants, and businesses. Located in Tshiombo, Limpopo, we’ve grown from a small community supplier to a trusted brand across the region.

Our Mission & Values

Community First

We reinvest in our community through food parcels, school supplies, and youth support programs.

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly production and efficient water usage.

Trust

Our clients rely on us for reliability, transparency, and top-quality ice every time.

Our Team

We are more than a factory — we are a family. Our team includes skilled youth, community leaders, and experienced staff who work tirelessly to keep Venda cool.
