Our Story & Mission

MURN Ice Cubes & Blocks Factory was established to provide dependable, food-safe ice solutions across Venda. Our mission focuses on delivering consistent, purified ice while investing in the region through employment and training programs. We believe a local business can be a positive anchor for economic opportunity while serving the practical refrigeration needs of the community, businesses, and events.

Manufacturing Standards

Production follows documented SOPs—water filtration, controlled freezing, hygienic handling and packaging. Quality assurance inspections include clarity checks and routine microbiological testing. Packaging uses food-safe film and tamper-proof seals for customer confidence.

Our Team

Our staff are trained in hygiene, equipment operation and logistics. We combine veteran manufacturing managers with locally recruited operators and drivers. The leadership group maintains transparent production logs and implements continuous training plans for staff skill development.

Our Commitments

Quality & Hygiene

Strict protocols for clean production and packaging to ensure safe ice for consumption.

Local Employment

We prioritise hiring and training local youth to build skills and provide stable jobs.

Community Support

We organise donation drives and partner with local schools and clinics.

Qualifications & Safety

All production staff are trained in food safety and hygiene. MURN maintains production logs, equipment calibration records and adheres to recommended public health guidelines. Our commitment to safety extends to staff PPE, workplace sanitization, and regular audits.
