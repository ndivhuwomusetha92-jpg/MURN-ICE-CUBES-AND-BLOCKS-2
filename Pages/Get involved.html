Get Involved — Partner with Purpose

MURN values partnerships that deliver measurable community outcomes. There are multiple ways to get involved — whether you are an individual who wants to volunteer, a business seeking a partnership, or an organisation looking to sponsor training. Your involvement supports jobs, skill development and improved access to essential cooling services in our region.

Volunteer Programs

Volunteer roles include packaging, event logistics, mentoring in skills workshops, and assisting with outreach. Volunteers receive a certificate of participation and priority access to future training and events.

Donations & Sponsorship

Donations fund equipment maintenance, youth stipends and community events. Sponsorship packages can be tailored to corporate partners, including co-branding at events and priority supplier status.

Corporate Partnerships

Businesses can partner on supply contracts, co-sponsored community programmes or logistics support. We build long-term relationships to support reliable supply chains and shared value projects.

Training & Apprenticeships

MURN runs hands-on training modules covering production operations, hygiene, logistics and basic business skills. Sponsors can fund cohorts or provide apprenticeship placements.
