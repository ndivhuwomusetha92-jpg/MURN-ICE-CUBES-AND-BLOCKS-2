About Our Factory

MURN Ice operates a hygienic, modern production facility in Tshiombo. We supply crystal-clear ice cubes for hospitality and strong ice blocks for storage and transport. Our processes are built on careful filtration, controlled freezing, and rigorous handling procedures to ensure product purity. We take pride in timely logistics, professional packaging, and an unwavering commitment to community upliftment through training and employment.

Crystal-clear Cubes

Precision-made cubes for restaurants, bars and events. Each batch is inspected for clarity and packaged hygienically using food-grade materials.

Strong Blocks

High-density blocks designed for transport and long-duration cooling. Ideal for large events, cold-chain needs, and commercial cooling tasks.

Reliable Delivery

Daily delivery across Venda with flexible scheduling for businesses and event planners. We ensure the right volumes arrive at the right time.

Community Impact

We invest profits into training programs and local initiatives. Buying from MURN supports youth skills development and local economic growth.
