What We Do

At MURN ICE FACTORY, we specialize in manufacturing and distributing premium ice cubes and blocks for both domestic and commercial use. Beyond business, we are committed to empowering youth, supporting education, and uplifting our local community.

Key Areas of Our Work

Ice Production

Producing crystal-clear ice cubes and long-lasting blocks, ensuring quality and reliability for all customers.

Distribution

Fast delivery across Venda, supplying households, restaurants, and events with premium ice.

Community Outreach

Donating food parcels, clothes, and school supplies while supporting disadvantaged families.

Youth Empowerment

Providing jobs, career advice, and mentorship to young people in the community.

Our Impact

Over the years, MURN ICE CUBES & BLOCKS has grown into more than a business. We are a pillar of support in our community — creating jobs, inspiring the next generation, and ensuring that no one is left behind.

Selected Projects & Case Studies

We prioritise quality over quantity — a small number of high-impact projects demonstrate our operational excellence, reliability and social value. Below are three representative examples of our work across events, foodservice partnerships, and community programmes that show how MURN adapts to different scales and requirements.

Wedding & Event Supply

Case: Weekend wedding for 500 guests. We provided chilled cubes, block reserves and on-site storage with two timed deliveries. The event required a bespoke delivery plan and temporary cold storage. Result: zero delivery incidents, satisfied host and repeat business.

Restaurant & Retail Partnerships

Case: Ongoing weekly supply to a mid-sized restaurant chain. We coordinated weekly pick-up windows, provided invoicing and quality guarantees. This partnership reduced ice-related stockouts and improved consistency during peak service hours.

Community Drives

Case: Local food preservation initiative. We supplied blocks and cubes to assist community vendors and clinics with preservation of perishable items. The project improved access to refrigeration support and helped local traders reduce spoilage losses.
