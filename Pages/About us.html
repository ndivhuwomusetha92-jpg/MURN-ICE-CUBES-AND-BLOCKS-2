About MURN ICE CUBES & BLOCKS FACTORY

Delivering quality ice while creating local opportunities in Tshiombo and across Venda.

Our Story & Mission

MURN ICE began as a community micro-business and has grown into a reliable supplier offering jobs and training opportunities. Our mission is to produce safe, high-quality ice while reinvesting in our community through donations, trainings and partnerships.
